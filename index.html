About This Site

On this site you will discover the countries in which I have lived. You'll learn more about Guinea and Peru, their geography, culture, gastronomy and tourism. I lived in Guinea, in Conakry between 2012 and 2014, as part of a humanitarian family trip. I lived in Peru, in Lima, in 2023 as part of a university exchange with IESEG.
